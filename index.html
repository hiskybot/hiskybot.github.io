ABOUT US

Half Sky Intelligence specializes in the research and development of bionic robots. Its products are primarily used for cleaning glass curtain walls in commercial buildings and aerial cleaning, addressing key challenges such as labor shortages, low efficiency, and high risks in exterior wall and aerial cleaning operations.

From its inception, Half Sky Intelligence secured strategic investment from Gaoxinqi Group. Currently engaging in capital operations, we aim to become a publicly listed entity within five years, with the vision of benefiting humanity through robotics.

Half Sky Intelligence Bionic Commercial Glass Curtain Wall Cleaning Robot

ROBOTS

Half Sky Intelligence’s flagship product is a bionic commercial glass curtain wall cleaning robot. Leveraging bionic robotics technology, it tackles the challenge of overcoming obstacles in glass curtain wall cleaning, while autonomously detecting obstacles and glass stains to achieve self-guided navigation.
